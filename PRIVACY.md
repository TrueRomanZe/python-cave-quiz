# Privacy Policy - The Python Cave

**Last Updated: March 18, 2026**

## Our Commitment to Privacy

The Python Cave is a free, educational quiz game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any data we collect.

## What We Collect

This application uses **Google Analytics** to understand how people use the game and what features are most helpful. This helps us improve the experience.

### Data We DO Collect (Anonymously)

- **Page views**: Which questions you visit
- **Session duration**: How long you play
- **Device type**: Mobile, tablet, or desktop
- **Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.
- **General location**: Country and region (not precise location)

All this information is **aggregated and anonymous** - we never identify individual users.

### Data We DO NOT Collect

- ❌ Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
- ❌ Your precise GPS location
- ❌ Your answers or quiz scores (these stay in your browser only)
- ❌ Any account information (no accounts required!)
- ❌ Passwords or sensitive data

## IP Anonymization

Google Analytics is configured to **automatically anonymize your IP address**. This means:
- Part of your IP is removed before storage
- We cannot identify you personally
- Your privacy is protected

## No Data Selling

We **NEVER**:
- Sell your data
- Share data with third parties (except Google Analytics)
- Create user profiles
- Track you across websites
- Use data for advertising

## Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies (small text files) to:
- Distinguish unique visitors
- Remember your session

**These cookies do NOT store personal information.**

### How to Opt Out

If you prefer not to be tracked, you have several options:

**Option 1: Use Incognito/Private Mode**
- All browsers have a private mode that doesn't save cookies

**Option 2: Install a Tracker Blocker**
- Browser extensions like "uBlock Origin" or "Privacy Badger"

**Option 3: Google Analytics Opt-Out**
- Install the [official Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

**Option 4: Block Third-Party Cookies**
- Configure your browser to block all third-party cookies

## Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for **2 months**, after which it is automatically deleted. Only aggregated statistics (that cannot be linked to individuals) are kept longer.

## Children's Privacy

This game is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## GDPR & Legal Compliance

This app complies with:
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation - European Union)
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- International privacy best practices

### Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:
- Access your data (though we don't collect identifiable data)
- Request data deletion
- Object to data processing
- Data portability

Since we don't collect personal identifiable information, there's no specific data about you that we can provide or delete. However, you can always use the opt-out methods above.
